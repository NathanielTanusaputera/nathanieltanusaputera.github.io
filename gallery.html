OUR PALLETE IN A GLANCE

Moments capture in time, bursting with the colors of foods in the making coupled with the taste of traditional. Our gallery is completed by a collection of images that could better illustrate the process, hard work, and taste of our home. From scaling a salmon to the environment of our home, Tako Grill provides the atmosphere in which people would experience. Pictures are one of the ways to show emotion, mood, and art. We want this gallery to show what we are trying to convey, welcoming a guest to a special journey, a trip to a Japanese home.

One of the ways that feelings, moods, and artistic expression can be shown is via the use of pictures. We hope that this gallery will be able to demonstrate what it is that we are attempting to communicate, which is the idea of inviting a visitor on an extraordinary excursion, namely a visit to a Japanese home.
